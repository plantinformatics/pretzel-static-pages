Barley Pretzel down for scheduled maintenance

As part of our plans to upgrade and integrate Barley Pretzel into the newer Pretzel that contains data from the Australia Grains Genebank (AGG) Strategic Parnership, Barley Pretzel will be temporarily unavailable this holiday season, with the plan to resume service in 2026.

In the meantime, you can continue to work in Pretzel with the latest functionality and AGG datasets here: agg.plantinformatics.io

If you require assistance moving datasets into the new Pretzel, please contact Kerrie Forrest kerrie.forrest@agriculture.vic.gov.au.
