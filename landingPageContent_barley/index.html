Pretzel Barley

An interactive, web-based environment for navigating multi-dimensional datasets in barley, including genetic maps and chromosome-scale physical assemblies.


Currently under development. Researchers interested in beta testing are invited to sign up for an account.

Users can explore the pre-loaded datasets as well as upload their own content.

A user guide is available here.

Please contact info@plantinformatics.io with any questions or queries.
